Hobbies

What I Like to Do For Fun

Currently, I enjoy watching movies, hanging out with friends and working out. I love to watch different movies that I have had recommended to me. I have a list of movies on my phone that is almost like a "to do" list of watching. I enjoy spending time with my roomates and other peers from Gonzaga. I also enjoy working out in the gym at my apartment complex or going to play basketball with my friends.

Here is the trailer for one of my favorite movies, Goodfellas.

Another thing that I love to do around campus is play intermural sports. In the past I have played intermural basketball, football, softball, and soccer. I believe that it is a great way to stay in shape and spend time with friends.

When I was younger I played organized lacrosse, soccer and tennis. While I do not play organized sports at Gonzaga, I still love to get outside with my friends and kick a ball around or play catch.

Recently, I have enjoyed playing chess. It is something that I have been playing with my dad ever since I was in elementary school. While it can be challenging and frustrating at times, I believe that it is a great game that tests your intelligence and makes you think quickly.
